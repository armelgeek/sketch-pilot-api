## Animation Characteristics
1. **Pacing**: Rapid scene changes (approx. every 10 frames).
2. **Text Usage**: Subtitles are consistently placed at the bottom center or integrated into the scene.
3. **Contrast**: High contrast between black drawing and warm background.
4. **Composition**: Center-focused for single characters, split-screen for comparisons or mixed media (Skinner scene).

## Implications for System Update
- Need support for "Collage" layouts (Scene 5).
- Need support for "Mixed Media" overlays (Scene 9).
- Transition engine should handle rapid cuts (Scene-per-subtitle model).
- Character positioning should be flexible (Center, Left/Right for comparisons).
