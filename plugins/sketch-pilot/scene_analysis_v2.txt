## Style Summary (v2)
- **Animation Speed:** Very fast cuts (~1 second per scene).
- **Compositional Variety:** High. Moves from single character to split-screen to complex diagrams to mixed-media.
- **Visual Shorthand:** Heavy use of thought bubbles, labels, and percentage markers.
- **Color Accent:** Subtle (mostly B&W with gray shades, but allows for potential red highlights like in Batch 1).
